THE COMPANY

DaraCo Technologies is a custom software development company that has been creating complex business-driven solutions for more than half a decade. Our approach is based on sharing the client's values, aligning workflows into the business processes of our partners, and striving for mutual understanding at every stage of interaction. We prioritize excellent communication with customers and stakeholders.

Our core expertise includes Logistics, Healthcare, FinTech, Agriculture, E-Commerce, Real Estate, Media, and Telecom.

We have only an in-house team and do not hire contractors. Our team consists of highly qualified professionals that produce outstanding products for our clients.
